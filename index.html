Why Choose Us

Your satisfaction is our priority.

📸Hello, I'm Erick! Photography is what I do. You may wonder what motivated me want to become a photographer. Well, I grew up in a few different towns. I was exposed to several cultures, which fueled my passion to capture them.

The inspiration drawn from those experiences led me down a path to where I am today, with more than 5 years of experience and a desire to contribute to the global cultural landscape.

I currently run a Studio, MightyPics Photography in Mwatate Town, Taita-Taveta County, Kenya.
